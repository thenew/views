Last year, I [ranked 56 films expectations](http://views.thenew.fr/posts/2021-films-expectations/) so it’s time to update it. I watched 30 (!) of these films and almost all of them legally. 12 have been delayed to 2022 (two were already listed for the [ranked in my expectations last year](2020 films](http://views.thenew.fr/posts/2020-films-expectations/): *Morbius* and *Top Gun: Maverick*).
I only missed 14 of them, some because of the closed cinemas but most of them for lack of desire (*F9* and *Venom 2*, I’m looking at you).

On every review, next to the title, I indicate the predicted rank and the evolution with the final rank.
