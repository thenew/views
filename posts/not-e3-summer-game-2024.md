Once again, I list my favourite video games trailers from all the conferences of the not-e3/Summer Game Fest.

It was a timid year: Nintendo came late to the party with a short but good list of announcements, Sony was even quieter, Xbox kept its strategy to throw a lot of games, but nothing spectacular, and finally, Ubisoft brought back the old school format of speakers on stage with live music aimed directly at me: _Star Wars_ and Japanese taiko drums for the new _Assassin's Creed_.
But what really shined were the "small" conferences, like the Wholesome Direct and Latin America Games Showcase, which became very good.

</div>
</div>

This year, _Steam_ prepared a neat [overview page](https://store.steampowered.com/sale/summer_showcase_2024) of the event to find all the games showcased.
